## A boutique firm with enterprise impact

The Big Data Company is a six-person core team of data scientists, AI engineers and geospatial specialists. We were founded in 2015 on the belief that AI in practice is mostly about **good craftsmanship**: not hype. Our customers choose us because we work transparently, deliver quickly, and stick around to make models better every year.

We work with multinationals like **McDonald's**, **SPAR** and **EssilorLuxottica**, and with scale-ups making their first data investment. Same quality, same models, only the format differs.

## Our expertise

- **Geospatial AI**: our main specialty. We have the most accurate spatial data foundation in Europe and use transformer models to understand locations the way language models understand words.
- **Custom forecasting**: revenue, demand, risk. Statistical models strengthened with machine learning, always transparent and explainable.
- **Computer vision**: storefront recognition, product detection, and automation of fieldwork.
- **Data engineering & MLOps**: scalable pipelines and model deployments that still work two years from now.

## How we work

> Custom-built, transparent and robust. We build statistical models strengthened with machine learning, complemented by our clients' domain knowledge. We stay engaged to improve algorithms year over year.

We work in short sprints. We show first results within four weeks. We don't deliver a black box: you get insight into why a model predicts what it predicts, and you can steer it yourself.
