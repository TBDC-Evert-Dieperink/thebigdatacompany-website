## Geospatial AI

Our main specialty. We help retailers, QSR chains and logistics players better understand where their customers are, where competitors sit, and where the next profitable location belongs.

- **Location forecasting** — revenue forecasts for new and existing sites, up to 90% accurate.
- **Whitespot analysis** — find areas of unmet demand before competitors do.
- **Catchment analysis** — catchment areas, demographics, competitive density.
- **Network optimisation** — cannibalisation simulations, closure recommendations, reallocation.
- **Look-alike identification** — find locations that resemble your best-performing sites.

## Custom data science

Forecasting, classification, optimisation. We build the model around your problem, not the other way around.

- **Revenue forecasting** — for retail, QSR, logistics, last-mile.
- **Risk models** — vacancy, churn, fraud.
- **Demand forecasting** — inventory management, capacity planning.
- **Optimisation** — network, routes, pricing.

We build statistical models strengthened with machine learning. Always explainable, always in collaboration with your domain experts.

## Computer vision

Image recognition and geospatial imagery analysis — useful where manual fieldwork becomes too expensive or too slow.

- **Storefront recognition** — banners, names, opening hours from street imagery.
- **Product detection** — shelf monitoring, planogram compliance.
- **Geo-imagery** — satellite image classification for land use and building type.

## AI strategy & consulting

Not every problem needs an AI solution. We help you decide where AI genuinely adds value — and where a good dashboard, a better database, or simply a conversation with your team is the wiser choice.

- **Data audit** — what you have, what's missing, what can go.
- **AI strategy** — roadmap, prioritisation, business case.
- **Proof-of-concepts** — 4–16 hour sprint to validate a hypothesis.
- **MLOps** — production model architecture, deployment, monitoring.
